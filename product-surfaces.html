Shadow Scale

Subtle shadows for elevation. Use sparingly to maintain the flat, printed aesthetic.

shadow-sm

Subtle lift. Buttons, inputs.

shadow

Default shadow. Cards, dropdowns.

shadow-md

Medium lift. Modals, popovers.

shadow-lg

Strong lift. Dialogs, tooltips.

Prefer borders over shadows. The design system favors crisp 2px borders for definition rather than heavy shadows. Shadows are reserved for truly elevated elements like modals and dropdowns.
