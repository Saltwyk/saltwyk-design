Part 3: Brand Imagery

Sepia-toned intaglio illustration style for marketing and brand communications

Sepia Intaglio Illustration Style

Style Characteristics

  • ✓ Currency engraving aesthetic — Fine crosshatching, stippling, and line work reminiscent of banknote illustrations
  • ✓ Sepia/warm brown tones — Monochromatic palette using sepia scale (sepia-100 through sepia-700)
  • ✓ Ornate decorative borders — Guilloche patterns, scrollwork frames evoking security printing
  • ✓ Allegorical subjects — Scales of justice, handshakes, commerce symbols, gift boxes, coins/points
  • ✓ Paper texture background — Illustrations sit on sepia-50/sepia-100 "aged paper" backgrounds

Usage Guidelines

  • → Hero images — Large illustrations for landing pages, pitch decks
  • → Section dividers — Horizontal illustrations breaking up content
  • → Feature callouts — Small vignettes illustrating concepts
  • → Investor materials — Pitch deck illustrations, one-pagers
  • ⚠ Not for product UI — Too heavy for dashboard/checkout flows
