Join the Network

You're trapped between growth and profit.

Current acquisition channels force you to choose: Spend more on ads and discounts to grow, or protect your margin and stagnate. Credit cards extract fees either way. The system isn't designed to help you prosper. It's designed to extract from you.

Breaking free requires seeing points differently.

Graph showing margin erosion from discounting and competitive pricing pressure

Points commerce is the path to prosperity.

For merchants willing to shift their perspective on what points are for, there's a way out. Shoppers spending points aren't hunting for discounts or comparing prices with AI agents. They're spending found money.

Higher order values. Better conversion. Premium customers. Better economics than credit cards.

While AI shopping agents race everyone to the bottom, points commerce sets you apart.

Comparison showing better unit economics with points vs traditional discounting

Higher Order Values

Shoppers spending "found money" focus on experience. They buy what they want with less price sensitivity.

New Customers

Premium customers from the network who you wouldn't have acquired otherwise.

Better Economics

Cards, PayPal, and Klarna extract from shopper cash. Points activate earned value. You keep more of every sale.

How it works

1

Shoppers connect their accounts

2

Points become payment

3

You get paid in USD

We handle settlement, reconciliation, and compliance. You focus on selling. Transparent economics.

4

Your points program drives partner growth

Use points to build cross-merchant campaigns and strategic partnerships. Turn customer acquisition into a network effect.
