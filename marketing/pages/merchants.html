For Merchants

Points Commerce: A new channel for growth.

Get Started

[Illustration: Merchant Dashboard]

Point acceptance interface showing incoming payments and customer metrics

Discounts erode margin. Credit cards take 3%. Shopping agents race prices to the bottom.

Every customer acquisition channel costs more. Every sale requires deeper discounting.

Merchants need a channel that doesn't depend on who can afford to lose the most margin. Merchants need a path to prosperity.

Points commerce is that path.

When shoppers pay with points instead of credit cards, they're spending found money. Not hunting for discounts. Not comparing prices across tabs.

That psychology changes the transaction. Higher order values. Better conversion. No discounting required.

You get new, premium customers you wouldn't have acquired otherwise, at economics that actually work.

Higher Order Values
