Section Flow

1

Navigation + Thread

2

Hero (Tropic Gradient Surface)

Thread-tropic line, gradient-tropic-bg, gradient text headline, centered content. "Become a Partner" CTA.

3

Partner Value Proposition (Ledger + Two Cards)

Two side-by-side white cards on ledger. Left card (cool thread accent): client focus. Right card (flame thread accent): partner focus. Fraunces emphasis on key phrases.

4

What Partners Offer (Ledger)

Text left (60%), illustration right (40%) on ledger background

5

How It Works (White)

Illustration left (40%), text right (60%). Alternating layout.

6

Partner Economics (Tropic BG)

Solid intaglio-900 headline, 3-column economics grid, italic closing note

7

Who This Is For (Ledger)

2x2 grid of partner type cards with white backgrounds

8

CTA Section (Flame + Texture)

Warm-tinted diagonal lines over flame gradient. "Let's Talk" inverse button.

9

Footer (Dark)

Brand, links, legal with spectrum thread above
