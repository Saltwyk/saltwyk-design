Page Specification

About Page

Bold declarative approach. Four self-contained belief statements with varied headline sizes (52–80px) and alternating backgrounds. Two-card treatment, dark intaglio section, emphasis callout with credit card counter-positioning. Builds to single powerful finale: "We're building the trillion point economy."

Manifesto Design Decisions

  • Four declarations + finale: Each section makes one bold, self-contained statement. Varied headline sizes (52–80px) create visual hierarchy. No redundant dark sections — single powerful finale.
  • Alternating backgrounds: Spectrum → Ledger (cards) → Dark intaglio → Ledger (callout) → White (name + thread) → Dark emerald finale.
  • Two-card treatment: Statement 2 splits shopper/merchant perspectives into side-by-side cards with 6px thread accent borders (cool + flame), visible shadows.
  • Dark Statement 3: Intaglio-800→900 gradient with -45° line texture. White text. No gradient text — boldness through contrast.
  • Emphasis callout: Statement 4 counter-positions against credit cards. Emphasis: "When both sides win, no one needs to extract." — 32px, emerald-300 borders.
  • Name section: Spectrum thread at top, "OUR NAME" heading in emerald-600, clean text on white (no box). Declarative, not questioning.
