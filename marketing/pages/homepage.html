Page Specification

Homepage

The main entry point for Saltwyk.com. Marketing gradient hero with dramatic emerald-to-intaglio treatment. The message IS the hero.

Galaxy Opal Design Decisions

  • Marketing Gradient Hero: Dark emerald-to-intaglio gradient creates drama and sophistication. The message IS the hero - no competing illustration needed.
  • Single CTA: "Join the Network" works for both merchants and partners. White button stands out against dark gradient.
  • Alternating Layouts: Content sections alternate text/illustration positions to create visual rhythm.
  • Thread Discipline: Thread line at bottom of sticky nav - always visible on scroll. Restraint is part of the design language.
