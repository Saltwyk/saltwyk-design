Page Specification

Legal Pages

Shared template for Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Dark intaglio gradient hero, single-column long-form content at 700px max-width. No thread spectrum in hero — just the gradient. Same template, different content.

Legal Pages Design Decisions

  • Dark intaglio hero: 135deg gradient from intaglio-800 → intaglio-900 → intaglio-950 with subtle diagonal texture overlay (4% opacity). Intentionally subdued — legal pages don't compete for attention with product pages.
  • No thread spectrum: Legal pages omit the thread spectrum entirely. The dark intaglio hero transitions directly into ledger content, and the content flows directly into the dark footer. Keeps the page understated and unadorned.
  • 700px content width: Narrower than product pages for comfortable reading of long-form text. Standard 16px/18px body text with 1.7 line-height for readability.
  • Section headings: Fraunces 24px (mobile) / 32px (desktop) with 48px/64px top margin between sections. Enough breathing room for scannable legal content.
  • Links: emerald-600 with underline, standard interactive color treatment. Used for email links, cross-references between legal docs.
  • Shared template: Terms and Privacy use identical layout. Only the page title, "last updated" date, and body content differ. Both shown below as separate device frame states.

Page Structure

Section Flow

1

Navigation (Overlaid on Hero)

Transparent nav over dark hero. Light logo + light nav links. "Let's Talk" CTA links to contact.html.

2

Hero (Dark Intaglio Gradient)

135deg gradient: intaglio-800 → intaglio-900 → intaglio-950. Diagonal texture at 4% opacity. White H1 title + warm-400 "Last updated" date. No thread spectrum in hero.

3

Content Body (Ledger BG)

700px max-width, centered. H2 section headings (Fraunces) + body paragraphs (Inter) + optional bulleted lists. Links in emerald-600.

4

Footer

Standard dark footer. No thread spectrum — content flows directly into footer.
