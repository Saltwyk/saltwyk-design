Page Specification

Calendar Page

Calendly embed page. Linked from the contact form success state. Post-booking redirects to meeting-confirmed.html.

Calendar Page Design Decisions

  • Separate page: Calendar booking lives on its own page, not embedded on the contact form page. This keeps the contact form focused and allows a clean post-booking redirect.
  • Calendly inline embed: Uses calendly-inline-widget for a seamless in-page experience. Calendly handles time selection, form collection, and scheduling.
  • Post-booking redirect: Configure Calendly to redirect to meeting-confirmed.html after successful booking.
  • Entry points: Linked from contact form success state ("Book time on my calendar") and potentially from nav or other CTAs.
