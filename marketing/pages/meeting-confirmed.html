Page Specification

Meeting Confirmed Page

Post-Calendly booking confirmation. Displayed after Calendly redirects back to the Saltwyk domain. Simple confirmation with back-to-home CTA.

Meeting Confirmed Design Decisions

  • Post-Calendly redirect: This page is the redirect target after a successful Calendly booking. Calendly handles its own confirmation screen first, then redirects here.
  • Simple confirmation: Checkmark icon, "Meeting Confirmed" headline, email reminder copy, and a single CTA to go back to the homepage.
  • No form, no embed: This is a terminal page in the flow. The only action is navigating back to the main site.
  • Flow: contact.html (success) → calendar.html (Calendly) → meeting-confirmed.html (this page).
