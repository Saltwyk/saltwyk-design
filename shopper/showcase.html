Usage Guidance

Cool Family

Cyan→Magenta gradients for celebration, delight, and general positive moments. The default shopper thread family.

Cyan→Lime gradients for success states, growth, and completion. Use for transaction confirmations and balance increases.

Flame Family

Orange→Magenta reserved for urgency—expiring points, limited offers. Use sparingly to maintain impact.
