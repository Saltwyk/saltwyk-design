Bright Opal

Shopper Product

Warm paper, soft thread washes—the "found money feeling" made visible.

Design Philosophy

Bright opal places soft thread gradients on warm, light surfaces. Where fire opal commands attention through contrast, bright opal invites trust through warmth. The shopper experience should feel like opening a wallet full of found money—rewarding, celebratory, approachable.

Light backgrounds dominate: ledger paper (#F5F2EB), white for cards, warm-50 and warm-100 for subtle variation. Thread gradients appear at 8% saturation for backgrounds, 20% for surfaces, and 40% for accents—never at full intensity. This creates an iridescent shimmer rather than a neon glow.

The shopper journey is: Discovery → Checkout → Celebration. Discovery should feel serendipitous—"Oh, I can use my points here?" Checkout should feel effortless—as quick as Apple Pay. Celebration should feel earned—you spent what you've already earned, not what you'll owe.

Motion reinforces this emotional arc. Use celebrate-pop for success moments, float for idle states that feel alive. Data density stays minimal—shoppers care about one thing: "What can I spend?"
