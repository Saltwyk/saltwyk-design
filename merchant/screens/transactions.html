Design Decisions

Selection Color: Navigation and filter active states use indigo (selection color) rather than emerald (action/success color). This maintains consistency with other selection patterns. Emerald is reserved for success states like the "completed" badge.

Fire Opal Treatment: The merchant dashboard uses the dark "Fire Opal" sidebar (intaglio-900) against the light "ledger" content area. The Cool thread gradient (cyan-thread → magenta-thread) provides professional authority for merchant identity.
