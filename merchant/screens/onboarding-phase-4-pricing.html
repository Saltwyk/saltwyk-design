3. Component Breakdown

Fee Structure Display

Component from /merchant/components/

  • Large rate display (48px)
  • Emerald color scheme for trust
  • Calculation examples
  • Links to pricing page and terms

Promotional Badge

Scarcity and urgency indicators

  • PROMO badge with emoji
  • Launch deadline clearly stated
  • Spots remaining (orange text)
  • Post-promo rate preview

Accept/Decline Flow

Promotional offer actions

  • "Decline Offer" shows confirmation modal
  • "Accept Offer" saves promo acceptance
  • Both proceed to Phase 5
  • Decision is final (no re-accept)

Standard Continue

No-promo flow

  • Single "Continue" button
  • No explicit acceptance required
  • Implicit agreement to terms
  • Proceeds to Phase 5
