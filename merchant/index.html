Fire Opal

Merchant Product

Dark matrix, bright threads—professional confidence for operational clarity.

Design Philosophy

Fire opal places bright thread gradients at full saturation against dark intaglio surfaces. Where bright opal invites through warmth, fire opal commands through contrast. The merchant experience should feel like a control room—professional, trustworthy, operationally confident.

Dark surfaces frame light content: intaglio-800 and intaglio-900 for sidebars and navigation chrome, ledger and white for the content area where merchants spend their attention. Thread lines appear at 100% saturation—they burn through the dark matrix rather than washing across it.

The merchant journey is: Onboarding → Operating → Prospering. Onboarding should feel fast—"I'm integrated in minutes, not days." Operating should feel clear—"I see exactly what's happening." Prospering should feel earned—"Your business is thriving because of decisions you made."

Motion is subtle and functional—no playfulness. Data density is high: transaction tables, metrics dashboards, status indicators. Use emerald for positive metrics (revenue, success rates), lime-thread for celebration moments, magenta and orange for alerts requiring attention.

The Contrast Principle

Dark chrome frames light content. The sidebar is always dark, the content area is always light. Thread lines at 100% appear only on dark surfaces—they would overwhelm light backgrounds.
