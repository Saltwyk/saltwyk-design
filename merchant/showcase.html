Usage Guidance

The Contrast Principle

  • • Sidebar is always dark (intaglio-900)
  • • Content area is always light (ledger/white)
  • • Thread lines at 100% only on dark surfaces
  • • Dark chrome frames light content

Thread Semantics

  • • Emerald — primary actions, revenue
  • • Lime — success, celebration, growth
  • • Magenta — errors, critical alerts
  • • Orange — warnings, attention needed
