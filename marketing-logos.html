Co-Branding Rules

When Saltwyk appears alongside partner logos in integrations, partnerships, or case studies.

Partner
✓

Correct: Equal Sizing

  • Match optical height, not pixel height
  • Use a vertical divider between logos
  • Maintain equal clear space
Partner
✗
✗

Incorrect: Unequal Sizing

  • Never make Saltwyk larger to "dominate"
  • Never make Saltwyk smaller to "defer"
  • Visual parity shows partnership equality

Logo Lock-up Order

On Partner Properties

Partner logo first, Saltwyk second

Neutral Context

Alphabetical order or by agreement

Animation Guidelines

Motion principles for the logo in digital contexts.

Approved Motions

  • ✓ Fade in (200-400ms ease-out)
  • ✓ Subtle scale (1.0 to 1.02 on hover)
  • ✓ Slide in from left (for nav)
  • ✓ Logomark rotation (loading states only)

Restricted Motions

  • ✗ Bounce or elastic easing
  • ✗ 3D transforms or perspective
  • ✗ Color transitions or morphing
  • ✗ Particle effects or disintegration
