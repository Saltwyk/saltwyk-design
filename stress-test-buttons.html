Evaluation Criteria

Visual Hierarchy

Does the primary button clearly stand out as the main action? Is there enough contrast between primary and secondary?

Brand Consistency

Does the strategy align with Saltwyk's emerald brand color? Does it feel cohesive with the design system?

Accessibility

Do buttons have sufficient contrast against backgrounds? Are interactive states clear on both light and dark surfaces?
