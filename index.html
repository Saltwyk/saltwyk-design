Design System

Version 0.6.0 · January 2026 · Full Reference

Intaglio Ink + Ledger Paper + Security Threads. The visual language of currency—engraved permanence, warm paper, fluorescent authentication—adapted for digital commerce.

Two treatments emerge from the same tokens: Fire Opal places bright threads against dark intaglio for merchant authority. Bright Opal washes soft thread tints across ledger paper for shopper approachability.
